about me:

I grew up in Indiana and attended Indiana University in Bloomington receiving majors in International Studies and Near East Languages and Cultures. After college I moved to Khamis Mushayt, Saudi Arabia to teach English at several schools and training centers in the 'Asir region. In 2017 I returned to the Midwest and have lived in Chicago since. Up until recently, I worked at Intelligentsia Coffee as a roaster and quality control specialist. Currently I am enrolled in Northwestern's coding bootcamp.
